Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Create a delicious meal of fried rice for you and the family to enjoy during the holidays or an special occasion.

Ingredients

  • Some pieces of Carrots
  • Buds of Green Peas
  • Spring Onions
  • Yellow Bell Peppers
  • Green Bell Peppers
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Black Pepper
  • Rice
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Butter/Magarine
  • Ginger
  • shrimp
  • Any other protein of your choice

    • Steps

    1. Cut all vegetables and prepare them for the sauce
    2. Wash your rice and leave it to parboil before putting it in a seive to drain water
    3. While the rice is parboiling, get ready to make the vegetable sauce
    4. Put Vegetable oil into a Frying pan and heat up lightly
    5. Turn all chopped Vegetables into the heated pan and stir fry for a while
    6. Add all spices into the frying pan till the sauce is to your tasting
    7. Remove the vegetable sause from the fire and turn it into a pot big enough to mix your rice
    8. Turn parboiled rice into Pot containing vegetables and turn on the heat on the pot
    9. Mix the rice and the sauce till a consistent texture is attained
    10. Add enough water to cook the rice to desired texture.
